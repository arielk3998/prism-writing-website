7. Monitoring and Measurement

7.1 Environmental Performance Indicators

We monitor and measure key characteristics of operations that can have significant environmental impacts:

  • Air Emissions: Continuous monitoring of stack emissions, monthly reporting
  • Water Quality: Daily sampling of discharge points, weekly laboratory analysis
  • Waste Generation: Monthly waste stream analysis and diversion tracking
  • Energy Consumption: Real-time energy monitoring, monthly efficiency reports
  • Resource Usage: Water, raw materials, and chemical consumption tracking
