1. Executive Summary

This Information Security Management System (ISMS) documentation establishes the framework for protecting organizational information assets in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 requirements. The ISMS provides systematic approach to managing sensitive company information through risk assessment, control implementation, and continuous improvement processes.

1.1 ISMS Scope

The ISMS covers all information processing facilities, systems, and services within [Organization Name], including:

  • Corporate IT infrastructure and cloud services
  • Customer data and intellectual property
  • Employee personal information and HR systems
  • Financial systems and payment processing
  • Third-party vendor and supplier relationships

2. Information Security Policy

Policy Statement: [Organization Name] is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all information assets through implementation of appropriate security controls and continuous improvement of our ISMS.

4. Security Controls Implementation

4.1 Annex A Control Categories

  • A.5 - Information Security Policies
    Establish management direction and support for information security
  • A.6 - Organization of Information Security
    Define security roles, responsibilities, and organizational structures
  • A.7 - Human Resource Security
    Ensure employees understand responsibilities and are suitable for roles
  • A.8 - Asset Management
    Identify, classify, and protect organizational assets
  • A.9 - Access Control
    Manage user access rights and prevent unauthorized system access
  • A.10 - Cryptography
    Ensure proper use of cryptographic controls to protect information
